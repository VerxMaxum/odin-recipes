Adobo Recipe

Photo by Serious Eats / Vicky Wasil

Description

This is one of the most influential Filipino food! It is composed of chicken meat marinated in soy sauce and vinegar, and there are other herbs in it, like garlic, bay leaves, and whole peppercorns. The taste of it is both salty and sweet. There are a lot of variations of this dish in different regions.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken
  • 3 pieces dried bay leaves
  • 8 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 4 tablespoons white vinegar
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon whole peppercorn

Steps

  1. Combine and mix chicke with soy sauce and garlic in a bowl. Marinate them for at least hour.
  2. Heat a cooking pot. Pour cooking oil.
  3. For 2 minutes on each side, pan fry the chicken when the pan is hot
  4. Pour the mixture a while ago into the pot and include the garlic. Add water to soften the chicken, then bring to a boil.
  5. Add bay leaves and whole peppercorns to the pot. Simmer until the chicken is tender.
  6. Add vinegar and stir it for 10 minutes.
  7. Put the salt and sugar. Turn off the heat after.
