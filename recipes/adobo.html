Adobo Recipe

Photo by Serious Eats / Vicky Wasil

Description

This is one of the most influential Filipino food! It is composed of chicken meat marinated in soy sauce and vinegar, and there are other herbs in it, like garlic, bay leaves, and whole peppercorns. The taste of it is both salty and sweet. There are a lot of variations of this dish in different regions.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken
  • 3 pieces dried bay leaves
  • 8 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 4 tablespoons white vinegar
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon whole peppercorn
