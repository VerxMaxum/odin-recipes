Mango Kani Salad Recipe

Photo by Recipedia

Description

Do you want to eat a salad that has mangos and (fake) crab? Well, here's the salad for you. This salad is acidic, fresh, and crunchy. It is complemented with crab sticks, or kani. You can top it with a dressing of your choice.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Lady's Choice Real Mayonnaise
  • 2 1/2 tbsp ketchup
  • 2 tbsp pickle relish (optional)
  • salt and sugar, to season
  • 2 pcs iceberg lettuce, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/4 pc red cabbage, sliced thinly
  • 3 pcs ripe mangoes, diced
  • 12 pcs Japanese crabsticks, shredded
  • 1 pc cucumber, cut into strips
  • 1 tsp tobiko fish roe
  • 1 pc nori, cut into strips
  • wonton wrappers, cut into strips and deep-fried

Steps

  1. Make the thousand island dressing from scratch. Mix well and preserve it in the fridge.
  2. Combine lettuce, cabbage, mangoes, crabsticks, and cucumber in a bowl. Drizzle it with the prepared thousand island dressing.
  3. Top it with tobiko, nori strips, and fried wonton. Enjoy!
