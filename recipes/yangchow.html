Yang Chow Recipe

Photo by Serious Eats / Vicky Wasil

Description

One of the best fried rice out there. It is mainly a yellowish fried rice that is cooked with chosen vegetables, and, more importantly, lots of egg. You can have it on your breakfast plate. Once it goes in your mouth, you will taste heaven.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 6 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 pc Knorr Shrimp Cube, mashed
  • 1-2 pcs Chinese chorizo or ham, chopped
  • 4 cups cooked, day-old rice
  • 1/4 cup green peas
  • 2-3 tbsp Knorr Liquid Seasoning
  • 2 pcs eggs, beaten, cooked, and chopped
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil (optional)
  • chopped spring onion and toasted garlic
  • 1/4 pc carrot, chopped
