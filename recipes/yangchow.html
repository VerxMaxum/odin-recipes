Yang Chow Recipe

Photo by Recipedia

Description

One of the best fried rice out there. It is mainly a yellowish fried rice that is cooked with chosen vegetables, and, more importantly, lots of egg. You can have it on your breakfast plate. Once it goes in your mouth, you will taste heaven.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 6 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 pc Knorr Shrimp Cube, mashed
  • 1-2 pcs Chinese chorizo or ham, chopped
  • 4 cups cooked, day-old rice
  • 1/4 cup green peas
  • 2-3 tbsp Knorr Liquid Seasoning
  • 2 pcs eggs, beaten, cooked, and chopped
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil (optional)
  • chopped spring onion and toasted garlic
  • 1/4 pc carrot, chopped

Steps

  1. Heat a pan, preferrably a wok over a medium flame. Add a drizzle of oil then saute the garlis until it's fragrant.
  2. Put the Knorr Shrimp Cube in and add the chorizo or ham. Saute them for 1 minute.
  3. Gradually, add rice, carrots, green peas, and Knorr Liquid Seasoning. Stir it for 2 minutes or more.
  4. Fold in the cooked eggs, and turn off the heat. Add some sesame oil then toss the pan.
  5. Transfer to a plate and enjoy!
